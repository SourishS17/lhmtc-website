Fundraising

Clay Pigeon Shoot

On 29th March 2014, some of our members took part in a Charity Clay Pigeon Shoot at Great Massingham. Great Fun and over £250.00 was raised.

The Three Peaks Challenge

On 30th June 2012, a group of intrepid Gentlemen decided to take on the challenge of conquering the summits of Ben Nevis, Scafell Pike and Snowdon - in a day! After hiking 25 miles and climbing up to 10,000 feet, they emerged very tired - but victorious! Over £3,000 was raised for the LHMTC - well done lads!

The Huntingdon 10k Run

On 17th June 2012, one of our Mums took part in this race to raise much-needed funds for the group. The event also marked a personal milestone!

Christmas Music Marathon

On 22nd December 2012, the LHMTC staged its 'Christmas Music Marathon'. Starting at the Vancouver Quarter, King's Lynn at 12.00pm, the group moved on to Hardwick and finished singing at 7.30pm!

Fundraising is the key to keep us going. If you have any ideas on fundraising events, please do get in touch!

But these alone are not enough - so please support us by sponsoring activities if you're a business looking to help theatre, or donate if you're an individual who wants to change lives!
